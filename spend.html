Project Spend

Project Spend uses official Treasury data to produce breakdowns of public expenditure in an easy to read and visual format. The following data views are currently available:

  • Spend Through Time: public expenditure through time, shown both in nominal GBP and as a percentage of GDP.
  • Spend by Department: public expenditure broken down by Government Department.
  • Economic Category Spend: public expenditure broken down by economic category (e.g. pay, procurement, grants).
  • Spend by Function: public expenditure broken down by functional area (e.g. Defence, Health, Education). Although similar to departmental spend, as departments are often defined by a functional area, this view tracks spend according to what the funds are spent on rather than which department spends the money.

Sources Note: All spend data on this website is sourced from HMT Public Expenditure Statistical Analyses (PESA) collections and OSCAR II transparency releases. For detailed information on the data, its limitations and definitions for terms you can visit: https://www.gov.uk/government/collections/public-expenditure-statistical-analyses-pesa .
